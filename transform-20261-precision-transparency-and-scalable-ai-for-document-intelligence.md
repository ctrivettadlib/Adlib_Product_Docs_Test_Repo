# Transform 2026.1: Precision, Transparency, and Scalable AI for Document Intelligence

Transform 2026.1 introduces major advancements that allow you to deploy document intelligence with greater confidence, flexibility, and scale. This release focuses on improving how you build, validate, and operationalize AI-driven document processing across enterprise environments.

You can now scale AI processing to meet real-world enterprise demand. With support for multiple model providers and configurations, including self-hosted and cloud-based models, Transform enables you to distribute workloads across models and environments. New scaling capabilities, such as model round-robin distribution, allow you to handle high-volume processing without hitting provider limits, ensuring consistent performance under load.

Transform 2026.1 also introduces a significantly enhanced Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) framework, integrated directly into workflows. You can now design workflows that include classification, review, approval, and structured data entry steps, and integrate external review systems. Workflows can pause, collect human input, and resume processing automatically, enabling complex, real-world business processes that require both AI and human decisions.

Building and validating extraction models is now faster and more intuitive. The new model builder allows you to generate models directly from a sample document, automatically proposing fields, rules, and structure. You can test extraction results within the same experience, compare outputs across multiple models, and version changes over time. This reduces setup effort and accelerates time to a reliable, production-ready model.

Transparency and trust in AI outputs are also strengthened. Transform now provides clearer insight into how results are generated through automated summaries and audit-level metadata. These capabilities help you understand extraction decisions, validate outputs, and support compliance requirements with greater confidence.

The platform architecture has been modernized to support faster and more scalable integrations. A new plugin-based integration framework simplifies how Transform connects to enterprise systems such as SharePoint and Exchange, reducing implementation effort and accelerating connector development.

Operational efficiency is improved through enhancements to job management and user experience. You can now retry jobs without re-ingesting documents, making it easier to iterate on configurations and workflows. Additional improvements to job visibility, document handling, and UI behavior reduce friction and speed up troubleshooting and development workflows.

Together, these advancements enable you to process documents more accurately, incorporate human decision-making where needed, integrate more seamlessly, and scale AI-powered workflows to meet enterprise demands.
